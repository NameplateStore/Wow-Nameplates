Do you ship internationally? How do I go about placing an order to an address outside India? What are the additional shipping charges? Will any additional customs duty and import charges be applicable?
Yes, we can ship internationally using DHL or FedEx. However, additional shipping charges would apply based on the actual fees charged to our account. You can complete your order on Urbanite Creation using a domestic address. At checkout, fill in the international address in the "Add a note to your order" section. When the order is ready, we will send you details of the applicable shipping fee along with a payment link. Once paid, your order will be shipped out. Most of our name plates weigh between 0.5 kg to 2 kg and shipping charges would vary from Rs. 1000 to Rs. 2500 depending on the weight of the shipment and the delivery location.
